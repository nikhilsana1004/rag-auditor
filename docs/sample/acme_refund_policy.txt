Acme Corp — Refund and Return Policy

Last updated: January 2025

1. Eligibility
Customers may return most items within 30 days of the purchase date for a full refund, provided the item is unused and in its original packaging. Items marked "Final Sale" at the time of purchase are not eligible for return or refund under any circumstances.

2. How to Initiate a Return
To start a return, log in to your Acme account and navigate to Order History. Select the order containing the item you wish to return, then click "Request Return." You will receive a prepaid shipping label by email within 2 business days. Pack the item securely and drop it off at any authorized shipping location.

3. Refund Processing
Once we receive and inspect the returned item, we will process your refund within 5–7 business days. Refunds are issued to the original payment method. Credit card refunds may take an additional 3–5 business days to appear on your statement, depending on your bank.

4. Damaged or Defective Items
If you receive a damaged or defective item, contact our support team at support@acme.com within 7 days of delivery. Include photos of the damage and your order number. We will arrange a replacement or full refund at no additional cost. You do not need to return the damaged item.

5. Digital Products
Digital downloads and software licenses are non-refundable once accessed or activated. If you encounter a technical issue, our support team will work to resolve it. If the issue cannot be resolved within 10 business days, a refund may be issued at our discretion.

6. Subscription Services
Monthly subscriptions may be cancelled at any time. Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing period — no partial refunds are issued for unused days. Annual subscribers may request a pro-rated refund within 14 days of their renewal date.

7. International Orders
International orders are subject to the same return policy, but customers are responsible for return shipping costs and any applicable customs fees. Refunds will be issued in the original currency; exchange rate fluctuations are not compensated.

8. Contact
For questions about your return or refund status, contact us at support@acme.com or call 1-800-ACME-HELP (Monday–Friday, 9am–6pm EST).
